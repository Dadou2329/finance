# 📊 Group Project - Financial Analysis  
## Visa vs Mastercard (Group 13)

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to perform a **comparative financial analysis** between two companies in the same industry:

- Visa  
- Mastercard  

You must determine **which company performs better financially and explain why**.

---

## 📁 Deliverables

### 1. Excel File (Required)
The Excel file must include:
- Raw financial data (Visa vs Mastercard)
- Calculations of all ratios
- Clear comparison tables
- One table with all ratios
- Columns: Ratio | Visa | Mastercard | Comment
- Clear formulas
- No unnecessary formatting

👉 This file proves your work and calculations.

---

### 2. Presentation (PowerPoint or PDF)
- Duration: **8–10 minutes**
- Clear and concise slides
- Focus on analysis, not raw data
- Max 6 slides
- Focus on 3-4 key insights only

Structure:
1. Introduction
2. Key difference 1
3. Key difference 2
4. Key difference 3
5. Conclusion

👉 Only **one group member submits both files on Blackboard before April 30**.

---

## 📊 Step 1: Collect Financial Data

Find financial data from:
- Official company websites (Investor Relations)
- SEC filings (10-K)

Documents to use:
- Income Statement
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow Statement

👉 Use the **most recent year available**.

---

## 🧮 Step 2: Calculate Financial Ratios

You must calculate and compare the following:

### 🔹 Financial Structure
- Total Debt / Total Assets
- Long-term Debt / Equity

### 🔹 Liquidity
- Current Ratio
- Quick Ratio

### 🔹 Profitability
- Net Profit Margin
- Return on Equity (ROE)
- Return on Assets (ROA)

### 🔹 Efficiency
- Asset Turnover
- Receivables Turnover
- Inventory Turnover
- Fixed Assets Turnover

### 🔹 Market Ratios
- Price / Earnings (P/E)
- Price to Book
- Dividend Payout

---

## 🧠 Step 3: Analysis (Most Important)

⚠️ Do NOT only present numbers.

For each important difference:
- What is the result?
- Why does it happen?
- What does it mean?

👉 Example:
- Higher ROE → better profitability or higher leverage
- Lower debt → less financial risk
- High dividend payout → focus on investors

---

## 🏢 Step 4: Company Research

Research both companies:
- Business model
- Strategy
- Growth evolution

👉 This helps explain financial differences.

---

## 📈 Step 5: Excel Structure

Recommended format:

| Ratio | Visa | Mastercard | Comment |
|------|------|-----------|---------|

👉 Keep it clean and easy to read.

---

## 🎤 Step 6: Presentation Structure

### 1. Introduction (1 min)
- Present both companies
- Objective of comparison

### 2. Analysis (6–7 min)
Focus on **3–4 key insights only**, for example:
- Profitability
- Debt structure
- Efficiency
- Market perception

### 3. Conclusion (1–2 min)
- Which company is better?
- Why?

---

## ⚠️ Key Expectations

The professor expects:
- Clear comparison
- Logical explanations
- Link between strategy and financial results

---

## 🚨 Mistakes to Avoid

- Only listing ratios without explanation
- Too much information
- No comparison
- Exceeding time limit
