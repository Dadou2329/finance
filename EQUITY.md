# Equity & Stockholders’ Equity – Clean Version

---

# 🏦 What is Equity?

Equity = Assets – Liabilities

👉 Represents the value belonging to shareholders

---

# 📊 Structure of Equity

## 1. Contributed Capital

Definition:
Money invested by shareholders

Includes:
- Common stock
- Preferred stock
- Additional paid-in capital (APIC)
- Treasury stock (negative)

👉 Source: company raises funds by issuing shares

---

## 2. Earned Capital

Definition:
Accumulated profits kept in the company

Includes:
- Retained Earnings
- Accumulated Other Comprehensive Income (AOCI)

👉 Retained Earnings = cumulative profits – dividends

---

# 📈 Key Concepts for Analysis

## Retained Earnings

Formula:
Retained Earnings = Past Profits – Dividends

Meaning:
- High → company reinvests profits
- Low → company distributes profits

---

## Dividends

Definition:
Payments to shareholders

Types:
- Cash dividends → reduce cash and equity
- Stock dividends → increase shares, reduce retained earnings

👉 Important:
- Not all companies pay dividends
- Depends on strategy

---

## Dividend Payout (IMPORTANT for your project)

Formula:
Dividend Payout = Dividends / Net Income

Meaning:
- High → returns cash to investors
- Low → reinvests in growth

---

# 📉 Treasury Stock (Buybacks)

Definition:
Company repurchases its own shares

Effects:
- Reduces equity
- Increases ratios like:
  - ROE
  - EPS

👉 Important insight:
Buybacks can artificially improve performance ratios

---

# 📊 Shares Structure

## Authorized Shares
Maximum shares allowed

## Issued Shares
Shares created by the company

## Outstanding Shares
Issued – Repurchased shares

👉 Important for:
- EPS
- valuation ratios

---

# 📊 Additional Paid-In Capital (APIC)

Definition:
Amount paid by investors above par value

Example:
If share price = $15 and par value = $1  
→ APIC = $14

---

# ⚠️ Important Insights for Analysis

## 1. Buybacks impact ratios
- Increase ROE artificially
- Reduce equity

👉 Be careful when comparing companies

---

## 2. Dividends reflect strategy
- High payout → mature company
- Low payout → growth company

---

## 3. Equity is not market value
- Based on accounting values
- Can differ from real company value

---
