# Liabilities – Clean Version

---

# 🏦 What are Liabilities?

Definition:
Liabilities = obligations a company must repay

👉 They are a source of financing

Accounting equation:
Assets = Liabilities + Equity

---

# 📊 Types of Liabilities

## 1. Current Liabilities (Short-term)

Definition:
Obligations due within 1 year

Examples:
- Accounts payable
- Accrued expenses
- Short-term debt
- Current portion of long-term debt

👉 Insight:
Used to finance day-to-day operations

---

## 2. Long-Term Liabilities

Definition:
Obligations due after more than 1 year

Examples:
- Bank loans
- Bonds
- Leases

👉 Insight:
Used to finance long-term assets

---

# ⚠️ Key Concept: Financial Leverage

Definition:
Using debt to finance assets

👉 More debt = more leverage = more risk

---

# 📊 Important Concepts for Your Project

## Debt Structure

Companies use:
- Short-term debt → operations
- Long-term debt → investments

👉 Good structure = match assets with liabilities

---

## Interest-Bearing Debt

Definition:
Debt that generates interest expense

Formula:
Interest Expense = Principal × Rate × Time

👉 Important for:
- Interest Coverage Ratio
- Financial risk analysis

---

## Interest Coverage (IMPORTANT)

Formula:
Interest Coverage = EBIT / Interest Expense

Meaning:
Ability to pay interest

👉 Higher = safer

---

# 📉 Key Risk Insight

👉 High debt = higher risk of bankruptcy

From the course:
- Too much leverage = dangerous
- Too little = inefficient

👉 Must compare with competitors

---

# 📊 Debt Ratios (IMPORTANT)

## Debt to Equity
Formula:
Debt to Equity = Total Liabilities / Equity

Meaning:
Level of leverage

👉 Higher = more risk

---

## Long-Term Debt to Equity
Formula:
LT Debt / Equity

Meaning:
Focus on long-term financing risk

👉 Higher = more risk

---

# ⚠️ Important Analytical Insights

## 1. Debt increases risk
- More interest payments
- Higher bankruptcy risk

---

## 2. Debt can improve returns
- Leverage increases ROE
- But increases risk

---

## 3. Compare companies carefully
👉 Debt levels vary by strategy and industry
