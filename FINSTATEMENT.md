# Financial Statements – Clean Version

---

# 🧠 Core Concept

Accounting Equation:
Assets = Liabilities + Equity

👉 Everything in financial analysis is based on this

---

# 📊 Balance Sheet

Definition:
Shows the financial position at a specific point in time

Structure:
- Assets
- Liabilities
- Equity

---

## Assets

Definition:
Resources that generate future benefits

Types:
- Current Assets → short-term (cash, inventory, receivables)
- Noncurrent Assets → long-term (PPE, intangibles)

---

## Liabilities

Definition:
Obligations the company must repay

Types:
- Current Liabilities → due within 1 year
- Long-term Liabilities → due after 1 year

---

## Equity

Definition:
Value belonging to shareholders

Includes:
- Contributed capital
- Retained earnings

---

# 📈 Income Statement

Definition:
Shows performance over a period

Structure:
Revenue  
– Cost of Goods Sold  
= Gross Profit  
– Expenses  
= Net Income  

---

## Key Concepts

### Revenue
Money earned from operations

### Expenses
Costs incurred to generate revenue

### Net Income
Profit of the company

---

# 🔗 Link Between Statements

👉 Net Income → increases Retained Earnings

Formula:
Retained Earnings = Beginning RE + Net Income – Dividends

---

# ⚠️ Accrual Accounting

Principle:
- Revenue recognized when earned
- Expenses recognized when incurred

👉 Not based on cash

---
